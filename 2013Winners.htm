2013 German Fest Stein Challenge Results

Thanks To All Who Participated in the 2013 German Fest Stein Challenge!

There were 242 entries and 169 registered participants, judges, and stewards.

Congratulations to Tom Gilbert!  His Dopple Bock has been chosen by the Milwaukee Brewing Company as the brew to be ramped up.
