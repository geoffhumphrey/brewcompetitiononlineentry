2011 German Fest Stein Challenge Results

Thanks To All Who Participated in the 2011 German Fest Stein Challenge!

There were 154 entries and 115 registered brewers, judges, and stewards.

Congratulations to Bob Manke!  His Bohemian Dunkel has been chosen by the Milwaukee Brewing Company as the brew to be ramped up.
